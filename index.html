Frequently Asked Question

How do i know the right course for me ?

We offer personalized guidance and assessments to help you choose the best course based on your interests and career goals.

Do i get a certificate for every course i take ?

Yes, upon completion of each course, you will receive a certificate that you can share with potential employers.

do i get refund if the course isn't worth my money ?

Yes, we have a refund policy that allows you to request a refund within a specified period if you're not satisfied with the course.

will i have lifetime access to course i purchase ?

Absolutely! Once you purchase a course, you will have lifetime access to all the materials and updates.

will you assist me to get my first job ?

Yes, we provide job placement assistance and resources to help you land your first job in the industry.

what are your payment options ?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for your convenience.

what are the requirement to start ?

To get started with our courses, you generally need a basic understanding of the subject area. Additionally, having access to a computer or device with an internet connection is essential for online learning.

Do i get all source code from projects ?

Yes, you will receive all the source code for the projects covered in the courses. This allows you to learn through hands-on experience and apply the knowledge gained to your own projects.
